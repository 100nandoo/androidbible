When you run this program, you will immediately see the look of the biblical text. Press the button below which shows the verse address (example: Genesis 1) to go to other parts of the Bible. Press the left button (or slide the trackball to the left) and right button to move to the chapters before and after.

Note: Some of the verses, especially in the book of Psalms, has numberings that differ between the Indonesian translation (or other languages) and other translations (e.g. KJV). The chapter and verse numbers of the specific translation are written in parentheses. For example, in Psalm 84 (Indonesian): 1 Untuk pemimpin biduan. Menurut... (84-2) Betapa disenangi tempat kediaman-Mu, ... 2 (84-3) Jiwaku hancur karena ... 3 (84-4) Bahkan burung pipit .... The ones colored green in this example are the chapter and verse numbering of the original Indonesian translation.

Moving to Other Parts of the Bible

To move to another part of the Bible, you can press the verse address button (example: Genesis 1) or press the MENU button to open the menu, then select Go to or Jump to (depending on the setting).

If No Navigation Buttons setting is enabled, you can press the gray verse address bar above the text instead of the verse address button.

Hold (long-press) the verse address button to open the list of recent verses.

Go to

With the Go to menu (or press the verse address button), you can choose the book, chapter, and verse you want to open. To select a book, press the book name, then you can choose one from the list of books. The books are ordered according to the original ordering (Old Testament then New Testament) or alphabetically, depending on the setting.

Jump to

With the Jump to menu (or press the verse address button, if it has been configured), you can directly type the verse address. How to write the verse address is: Write the first 3 or so characters of the book name, then the chapter number, then a space or a colon, then the verse number. For example Luk 3:11 or 1Pe 2:5 (1 Peter 2:5).

However, for your convenience, you can also write it in the following ways:

  • Type just the chapter number to go to the chapter on the same book. Example: 8
  • Type the chapter number followed by the verse number. Example: 3 16, 12:4
  • Type the book name sufficiently. Example: songs (Song of Solomon), 1k (1 Kings), ps (Psalms)
  • Concatenate the book name and the chapter number: Joh3, Gen48

Bookmarks

Hold (long press), any one verse to open the a menu (unless disabled in settings). On the menu there are options to Add Bookmark. With bookmarks, you can mark the necessary verses. You can also give a title to each bookmark. Go to the menu and select Bookmarks to see the list of bookmarks. Press one of the bookmarks to open it, or hold to change the title or to delete it.

By pressing the menu, you can also do Export to SD Card or Import from SD Card. Use this menu to ensure that your bookmaks and your annotations are stored properly in SD Card as a backup in case there is a problem with your phone.

Search

You can find verses that contain certain words. Press the search button (with the magnifying glass symbol) on your mobile phone if available, or open the menu and select Search.

When the search screen opens, type the words that want to look for and click the button. The verses that contains all the words you entered, will be displayed. For example, if you type god son, then even Genesis 4:25 would be displayed because it contains verses the word "god" and "son", although not contiguous. If you type just, you would find too "justice", "justify", etc.

  • Use quotes to search for exact phrases. For example, "our father".
  • Use plus sign (+) exactly before a word to search a whole word. For example, +eve will not find "even" or "evening".

Devotion

Open the menu and select Devotion to read some reflections which are provided every day. When you first open the devotion, you must be connected to Internet, because the devotion materials are available online. However, when you connect, the devotion materials of the day until about 15 days in the future, will be downloaded and stored so you do not need to be connected to the Internet to read the following days' devotions.

Press the Switch button to read devotions from different sources. Press the left arrow or right arrow buttons to move between days. Open the menu to copy the contents of the devotion or to share them with others.

Some devotions contain verse addresses that you can press to jump directly to the verses.

Version

You can read another version of the Bible (different languages or different translations) by selecting the Version menu. You need to download the data files of the additional versions when you select them for the first time. The files are stored on the SD Card. Each version will take approximately 5 MB. After you download it, you do not need to download it again next time.

Suggest

Open the menu and select Suggest to send a message, suggestion, criticism, idea, etc. to the makers of this program. Your writing will be sent when you connect to the Internet. We read every message that is sent, so we are looking forward to receiving your suggestions. Not all suggestions will be satisfied, however, but we will try to give you the best.

Settings

Open the menu and select Settings.

Some things that can be changed related to appearance:

  • Text Size for enlarging or reducing text size.
  • Font to change the type to Sans-serif, Serif, or Monospace.
  • Bold Text to use thicker text.
  • Color to change the colors of Bible text, the background, and the verse numbers.

Some things that can be changed related to usage:

  • Keep Screen On to let the screen always on when reading the Bible text.
  • Direct Address Input when checked, the Go to menu will change to Jump to, and when the verse address button is pressed, the Jump to screen will be displayed.
  • Sort Books Alphabetically when checked, the book selection on the Go to screen will show books in alphabetical order.
  • No Navigation Buttons when checked, the buttons below the Bible text will disappear. Instead there is a small bar on the top. You can press or hold the bar to navigate. You can press the left or right button on your phone to go between chapters.
  • Disable Verse Menu when checked, you will not be able to hold a verse to open the contextual menu. Check this if you often open the menu by mistake.
  • Volume Button Move when checked, you can go to the next/previous verses by pressing the sound volume buttons.

Context Menu

By holding down (long press) a verse, a menu will appear, and you can do several things:

  • Copy verse to copy the selected verse to be pasted into other applications.
  • Add bookmark to add a bookmark or edit a bookmark of the verse.
  • Write annotation to add an annotation of a verse. You will see a mark beside the verse so that you can see it later.
  • Highlight to put a highlighter color into the selected verse. The highlight data is stored together with bookmarks and annotations, so you can import and export them in the same way as Bookmarks.
  • Share to send the verse by email, bluetooth, Twitter, Facebook, etc. depending on the application installed on your phone.
